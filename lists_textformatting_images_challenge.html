Chicken Curry Recipe

Directions

In a non-reactive frying pan, heat the coconut oil. Add in the onions and place a dash of salt on top. Caramelize the chicken on a medium-low heat for 15-20 minutes.

After that, add in the chicken and brown. Before the browning is complete, add in the garlic and then the ginger; add in a pinch of salt. Add in the curry and the chilli flakes.

Pour enough coconut water to cover the meat, add a pinch of salt. Add in the tomatao paste, add a pinch of salt. Bring to a boil then reduce heat and cover.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 pounds of chicken
  • 2-3 cups of coconut milk
  • 1 whole onion
  • 1 can tamoto paste
  • 2-4 teaspoons of minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon agar
  • 1 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • 2 teaspoons organic curry powder
  • Salt and pepper
